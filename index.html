Building a More Equal Tunisia

The Tunisian Equality Network (TEN) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting equality and social justice in Tunisia. Founded on the principles of universal human rights, TEN works to create a more inclusive and equitable Tunisian society.

Our organization brings together activists, experts, researchers, and engaged citizens who believe in a future where every Tunisian, regardless of origin, gender, religion, or social status, can live with dignity and fully enjoy their rights.

Mission

Promote equal rights and opportunities for all Tunisian citizens through advocacy, education, and community action.

Vision

A Tunisia where equality, social justice, and respect for human rights are lived realities for everyone, every day.

Values

Equality, justice, dignity, inclusion, transparency, and civic engagement are at the heart of everything we do.
